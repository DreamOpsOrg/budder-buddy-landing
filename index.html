The Journey

Know what's normal.
Every single day.

Budder Buddy walks you through every phase so nothing feels scary or unknown.

01
Day 1–3
FRESH WOUND PHASE

Redness, swelling, and plasma weeping are completely normal. Budder Buddy keeps you on your wash and moisturize schedule and tells you exactly what you're seeing.

02
Day 4–7
PEELING BEGINS

The outer layer starts to flake. Your ink may look cloudy or dull — that's your skin healing over it. The app tells you exactly what's normal so you don't panic.

03
Week 2–3
SURFACE HEAL

The outer skin closes. It may feel tight and look slightly faded. UV protection becomes critical here. Your UV alerts are working hard for your investment.

04
Month 1–3
DEEP HEAL + TRUE COLORS

The deeper layers finish healing and your true ink colors emerge. Download your timelapse, share your journey, and start tracking your next piece.
