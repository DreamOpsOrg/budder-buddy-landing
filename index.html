The journey

Know what's normal.
Every single day.

Budder Buddy walks you through every phase of healing so nothing feels scary or unknown.

🩹
Day 1–3
Fresh Wound Phase
Redness, swelling, and plasma weeping are completely normal. Budder Buddy reminds you to keep it clean, stay moisturized, and resist the urge to touch it.
🌊
Day 4–7
Peeling Begins
The outer layer starts to flake. Your ink may look cloudy or dull — that's your skin healing over it. The app tells you exactly what's normal so you don't panic.
✨
Week 2–3
Surface Heal
The outer skin closes. It may still feel tight and look slightly faded. UV protection becomes critical here. Your UV alerts are working hard for you.
💎
Month 1–3
Deep Heal + True Colors
The deeper layers finish healing and your true ink colors emerge. Download your timelapse, share your journey, and start tracking your next piece.
