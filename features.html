01 / 06 Signature Feature

Ghost Camera

Consistency is everything when documenting a heal. Ghost Camera overlays a faded version of your very first check-in photo directly onto your live camera view, so you can perfectly align each new photo from the same angle, distance, and framing. No more guessing — every shot matches. When you stack them all together, the progression tells a real story: the swelling fading, the color settling, the skin closing. That only works when the angles are identical. Ghost Camera makes that effortless with zero setup or technical knowledge required.

How It Works

1

Take your first check-in photo — this becomes your reference frame stored in your tattoo's healing diary.

2

Open the camera for any future check-in and the ghost overlay appears at 40% opacity, showing exactly where your tattoo was in frame.

3

Align, snap, done. The new photo is saved to your diary and automatically queued for timelapse compilation.

Healing Timelapse

Every photo you take during your heal is automatically stitched into a smooth timelapse video the moment your healing period closes. Watch weeks of recovery compress into seconds — the swelling subside, the peeling lift, the vibrancy of the ink return. It's one of the most satisfying things you'll ever see. You can download the finished timelapse directly to your phone and share it anywhere, making it the perfect companion post when you debut your healed tattoo on social media. Your artist will love seeing it too.

How It Works

Check in consistently using Ghost Camera — the more check-ins you have, the smoother the timelapse will be.

2

App auto-compiles the video when your healing diary is marked complete, applying smooth crossfade transitions between frames.

3

Download and share — export as a vertical MP4 sized perfectly for Instagram Reels or TikTok with one tap.

03 / 06 AI-Powered

AI Healing Stage Guide

The middle of healing is confusing. Is that peeling normal? Should the redness be fading by now? Why does it look cloudy? Budder Buddy's AI analyzes your check-in photo and identifies exactly what stage of the healing process you're in — from initial inflammation to peeling, scabbing, clouding, and final settling. It tells you what's normal for where you are in the timeline, what signs warrant concern, and when it's appropriate to reach out to your artist. It's not a diagnosis, it's informed guidance from a system trained on the full spectrum of tattoo healing stages.

How It Works

1

Submit your check-in photo — tap "Analyze" and the AI examines color, texture, and surface detail in the image.

2

Stage identified instantly — the AI returns your current healing stage (e.g. "Stage 2: Active Peeling") with a plain-language explanation of what's happening under the skin.

3

Normal or Not flag — any detected anomaly (unusual swelling, color spreading, discharge) is flagged with a recommendation to contact your artist.

04 / 06 Smart Alerts

Live UV Rating

UV exposure is one of the most overlooked threats to a healing tattoo. Even indirect sun can break down fresh ink particles before they've fully bonded with the dermis, causing fading, blowouts, and uneven color retention. Budder Buddy pulls the real-time UV index for your location and surfaces it directly in the app, every day of your healing window. If the UV rating climbs into risky territory, you get an alert before you step outside — so you can cover up, stay in, or at minimum apply SPF with enough lead time to actually protect the work.

How It Works

1

Location access granted once — Budder Buddy uses your GPS coordinates to fetch the current UV index for your exact area, updated hourly.

2

UV dashboard in the app shows today's index, peak risk hours, and a simple risk level: Safe / Moderate / Cover Up / Stay In.

3

Morning push notification — if the day's forecast hits Moderate or above during your active healing window, you get alerted before you leave the house.

05 / 06 Stay On Track

Smart Reminders

Generic aftercare advice tells you to moisturize twice a day. Smart Reminders knows what day you're on, what healing stage the AI flagged, and what your skin needs right now. In the first 72 hours, wash reminders are more frequent to keep bacteria at bay. As you enter the peeling phase, moisturize reminders increase to prevent cracking and premature scab removal. Check-in reminders are timed around the best natural lighting windows of the day. Every notification is context-aware, not a generic ping — so you're never over-reminded or, worse, missing a critical care step.

How It Works

1

Log your tattoo date — Budder Buddy builds your personal care schedule automatically based on standard healing milestones for your tattoo placement.

2

Reminders adapt as you heal — when the AI detects a stage change, the reminder frequency and type update automatically without you touching a setting.

3

Snooze or log directly from the notification — tap to mark "done" or snooze 30 minutes, keeping your care log accurate with zero extra steps.

06 / 06 Knowledge Base

Ink Vault + Learn Hub

Every tattoo you've ever healed lives in your Ink Vault — a private, permanent archive of your entire ink journey. Each entry contains the full healing diary, every check-in photo, the timelapse, your care log, and any AI analysis notes from that heal. It becomes your personal reference for how your skin heals across different placements, styles, and seasons. Alongside the vault, the Learn Hub is a comprehensive, jargon-free guide covering every stage of healing, what normal peeling looks like versus signs of infection, the difference between an allergic reaction and standard irritation, and a complete aftercare do's and don'ts library you can access at 2am when you're panicking about a cloudy patch.

How It Works

1

Healing diaries archive automatically — once a healing period closes, the full record is sealed and stored in your Ink Vault forever, organized by date and placement.

2

Browse your collection anytime — tap any vault entry to revisit the day-by-day photos, timelapse, and care history for that piece.

3

Learn Hub is always one tap away — searchable articles, stage photos, and a symptom checker give you real answers without doomscrolling Reddit at midnight.
